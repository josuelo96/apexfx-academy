Forex is an over-the-counter market‚Äîno central exchange‚Äîwhere participants trade currencies via electronic networks. It exists to facilitate international trade, hedge currency risk, and allow speculation on price movements.

Currency Pairs & Spreads

Trading Sessions & Volatility

Forex runs 24 hr/day, five days/week, across four sessions: Sydney, Tokyo, London, New York. Overlaps (especially London/New York) see the tightest spreads and biggest moves.
